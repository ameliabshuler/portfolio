About Me

In mid-2023, I achieved a life-long dream of moving to Spain. I still keep a business presence in Northern Virginia and can bill US-based clients through my LLC. Physically, however, I am currently living in an apartment in Madrid where I have a visa that allows me to work outside of Spain.
