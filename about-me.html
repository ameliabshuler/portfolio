About Me

5-Year Plan

I know, I know. This seems super intense. But it is easier to have a five year plan when I’ve been dreaming of moving to Spain for 2+ years. In five years I hope to be living in Barcelona or Madrid working as a UX Designer for an international company.

To get there, I have been working diligently on my Spanish: I listen to a lot of ‘Reggaeton’ or Latin Pop music, watch Spanish movies (my favorite is Tenemos Que Hablar), and making as many Latinx friends as possible (in a non-creepy way I hope).

Love of DIY

I am weirdly (or not so weirdly, I am a designer) obsessed with problem-solving. Who needs a mechanic when you have YouTube? I have replaced sinks, dishwashers, tires (on a business trip!), and windshield wiper engines. There is nothing like the feeling of doing something yourself you’d otherwise be shelling lots of $$ out for.
